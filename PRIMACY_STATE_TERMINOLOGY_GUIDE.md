# Primacy State Terminology Guide

## Core Concept
**Primacy State (PS)** - The mathematical formalization of successful AI governance as a stable equilibrium condition.

**Formula**: PS = ρ_PA · (2·F_user·F_AI)/(F_user + F_AI)

---

## Complementary Terms for Different Audiences

### For Success States (PS → 1.0)

1. **Primacy Attractor Equilibrium (PAE)**
   - **Context**: Grant proposals, academic papers emphasizing innovation
   - **Why**: Sounds novel and technically sophisticated
   - **Example**: "Our system achieves Primacy Attractor Equilibrium through dual basin dynamics"

2. **Attractor Harmony**
   - **Context**: Interdisciplinary audiences, physics/dynamical systems conferences
   - **Why**: Intuitive and elegant, captures the synchronization aspect
   - **Example**: "Attractor Harmony ensures both constraints remain synchronized"

3. **Alignment Equilibrium**
   - **Context**: Control theory literature, regulatory documentation
   - **Why**: Directly connects to AI alignment discourse
   - **Example**: "We maintain Alignment Equilibrium through continuous monitoring"

4. **Governance Equilibrium**
   - **Context**: Policy briefs, compliance reports
   - **Why**: Clear focus on governance and control
   - **Example**: "The system maintains Governance Equilibrium across all sessions"

5. **Cooperative Stability**
   - **Context**: Game theory papers, multi-agent systems
   - **Why**: Established term in cooperative control
   - **Example**: "Dual PAs achieve Cooperative Stability through harmonic coupling"

### For Failure States (PS → 0)

**Attractor Decoupling**
- **Context**: Diagnostic reports, failure analysis
- **Why**: Clearly indicates loss of synchronization
- **Example**: "Attractor Decoupling occurred when ρ_PA dropped below 0.5"

### Related Degradation Terms

- **Purpose Drift** - When F_user degrades
- **Behavioral Violation** - When F_AI degrades
- **Cascade Failure** - When multiple components degrade
- **Governance Degradation** - General term for PS decline

## Key Insight

All these terms describe the same mathematical reality but resonate with different communities:
- **Mathematicians** appreciate the formalism
- **Physicists** understand the harmony metaphor
- **Engineers** connect with equilibrium concepts
- **Regulators** need governance language
- **Grant reviewers** want innovation framing

The τέλος (ultimate purpose) remains constant: achieving and maintaining PS = 1.0
