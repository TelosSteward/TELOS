**Key Insight:** The EU AI Act alone creates a $10B+ annual premium market by mandating coverage for high-risk AI systems across healthcare, finance, employment, and critical infrastructure.

### 1.2 The Insurability Gap

Today's carriers face a fundamental problem: **they cannot underwrite what they cannot measure.**

**Traditional Insurance Data:**
- Property: Square footage, construction type, location, claims history
- Auto: Driver age, vehicle type, miles driven, accident history
- Cyber: Network architecture, patch history, security controls

**AI Behavioral Risk Data:**
- How does the AI actually behave in deployment?
- When does it drift from intended purpose?
- What governance controls are active?
- How quickly are issues detected and corrected?

**No carrier has this data.** They're being asked to price behavioral risk with zero visibility into behavior.

### 1.3 The Historical Pattern

Every major insurance category emerged when someone built the data infrastructure to make risks quantifiable:

| Era | Risk Category | Data Layer Builder | Outcome |
|-----|---------------|-------------------|---------|
| 1970s-80s | Property | ITEL → Nearmap | $500B+ annual premium market |
| 2000s-10s | Cyber | Coalition, SecurityScorecard | $15B+ premium market, growing 25%+ annually |
| 2020s-30s | AI Behavioral | **TELOS** | Projected $50B+ by 2035 |

**The pattern is clear:** Risk categories don't become insurable until someone builds the measurement infrastructure. TELOS is that infrastructure for AI.

## 3. Business Model: The MGA Structure

### 3.1 What is an MGA?

A Managing General Agent (MGA) is a specialized insurance entity that:
- Underwrites policies on behalf of rated carriers
- Sets pricing and terms within delegated authority
- Handles policy administration and claims
- Earns commission on premium volume (15-25%)
- Does NOT require its own insurance license initially

**Why MGA First:**
- Faster to market (months vs. years for carrier license)
- Lower capital requirements ($5-10M vs. $50-100M+)
- Leverage existing carrier ratings and capacity
- Prove the model before full vertical integration
