## 2. SYSTEM OVERVIEW

TELOS is a runtime governance system for Large Language Models (LLMs) deployed in healthcare settings. It enforces regulatory compliance through mathematical controls, ensuring:

- GDPR Article 9 compliance for special category personal data
- Medical Device Regulation (MDR) alignment where applicable
- Prevention of Protected Health Information (PHI) disclosure
- Mitigation of medical misinformation risks

**Key Innovation:** TELOS achieves 0% Attack Success Rate through three-tier defense architecture (mathematical enforcement, policy retrieval, human oversight), validated across 84 adversarial tests.
