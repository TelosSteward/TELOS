## Primacy Dynamics Framework

### Core Concept

**Primacy = Human Authority Preserved in Semantic Space**

Primacy comprises:
- **Privacy**: Human control over information, boundaries, data usage
- **Purpose**: Human definition of intent, objectives, constraints
- **Authority**: Ultimate control exercised via human-in-the-loop governance

**Key Insight**: Primacy Dynamics inverts the typical AI architecture - AI becomes subsystem of human governance, not the other way around.

### The Primacy Attractor: Instantiated Constitutional Law

**The PA is the law of the land for session-level governance.**

Not merely a "measurement baseline" or "alignment target," the Primacy Attractor represents **human-authored constitutional requirements** that have legal force within ephemeral session state.

**How Constitutional Law is Instantiated**:
1. **Human-Authored**: Constitutional actors declare constraints for the session
2. **Instantiated**: Declared constraints become enforceable constitutional requirements
3. **Ephemeral**: Constitutional law exists within session state (not permanent model modification)
4. **Enforced**: The Constitutional Filter enforces this law in real-time through orchestration layer

**This Means**: When humans define boundaries, they're not setting "preferences" - they're **authoring constitutional law** for that governance context. TELOS enforces that law.

### Three-Tier Architecture

**Tier 3: Primacy Governor (Human Authority)**
- Domain experts exercise ultimate control
- **Author constitutional requirements** for session governance (privacy + purpose)
- Review escalated edge cases
- This is system design, not exception handling

**Tier 2: Governance Infrastructure (Automated Enforcement)**
- **Primacy Attractor = instantiated constitutional law** for the session
- Continuous fidelity measurement against constitutional requirements
- Basin tolerance violation detection
- Escalation when constitutional boundaries approached

**Tier 1: AI Subsystem (Constrained Operation)**
- LLM operates within primacy-defined bounds
- Generates responses under governance constraints
- Escalates when approaching boundaries
- Functions as tool under authority, not autonomous agent

### Information Flow

- **Upward**: Violations escalate to authority (Tier 1 → 2 → 3)
- **Downward**: Boundaries constrain operation (Tier 3 → 2 → 1)

**Principle**: Humans exercise governance, not "fixing AI mistakes." AI operates under constraints, not "in control."
