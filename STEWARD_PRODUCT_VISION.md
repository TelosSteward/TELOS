## The Steward Product

### Positioning

**The Steward: AI that curates purpose-driven recall**

### Tagline

**"Let Steward Explain."**

### Core Description

The Steward is AI that combines deep domain knowledge with purpose-aligned governance. It curates responses to stay on-topic, recalls information relevant to its purpose, and provides observable metrics proving alignment.

**Not just AI with knowledge. Not just AI with guardrails.**
**AI that curates purpose-driven recall.**

### What It Does

The Steward is a **Configurable AI Expert** for any domain:

1. **Domain Expert Defines Primacy Attractor**
   - Purpose: What should be explained
   - Scope: Boundaries of expertise
   - Boundaries: What to redirect away from

2. **Steward Embodies That Expertise**
   - Pre-established, fully calibrated
   - Natural conversation within domain
   - Graceful drift correction back to purpose

3. **Guaranteed On-Topic Guidance**
   - Handles ANY question
   - Always brings back to domain
   - No brittle keyword matching
   - Semantic understanding via TELOS

### Market Fit

**Ideal Use Cases (Non-Sensitive Data):**

- **Museum & Exhibit Guides** - Explain exhibits, handle visitor questions
- **Product Demonstrations** - Sales AI that stays focused on benefits
- **Educational Tutors** - Keep students on curriculum topics
- **Onboarding & Training** - Cover required materials thoroughly
- **FAQ & Knowledge Bases** - Expert answers within scope
- **Event Guides** - Conference/venue navigation and info
- **Brand Ambassadors** - On-brand messaging, every time

**NOT For (Requires Research Path):**
- Healthcare (sensitive, regulated)
- Financial advice (sensitive, regulated)
- Legal guidance (sensitive, regulated)
- Personal data handling (privacy-critical)
- Safety-critical systems (requires deeper validation)

### Why It Works

**The Steward = Domain Expert + Curator of Purpose-Driven Recall**

**vs. Traditional Chatbots:**
- ❌ Keyword matching → ✅ Semantic understanding
- ❌ Scripted responses → ✅ Natural conversation
- ❌ Brittle failure modes → ✅ Graceful redirection
- ❌ No real knowledge → ✅ Curated domain expertise

**vs. Generic AI:**
- ❌ Drifts off-topic → ✅ Curates purpose-aligned responses
- ❌ Recalls everything → ✅ Recalls only what serves the purpose
- ❌ Black box → ✅ Observable governance (Observatory)
- ❌ Ungrounded → ✅ Grounded in curated knowledge base

## Research Foundation (Grant Focus)

**This is the primary mission.** Product is secondary, emergent.

### Core Research Problem

**AI Governance for Purpose Alignment Across All Domains**

TELOS is infrastructure for:
- Critical systems (healthcare, safety, finance)
- Privacy-preserving governance
- Multi-stakeholder alignment
- Verifiable safety properties
- Regulatory compliance

### Why Research-First Approach Was Correct

1. **Infrastructure Before Application**
   - Built governance mathematics first
   - Validated with 45 studies
   - Established theoretical foundation
   - THEN discovered product applications

2. **Credibility & Trust**
   - Academic rigor → Grant funding
   - Validation studies → Regulatory acceptance
   - Open research → Community trust
   - Infrastructure → Broad applicability

3. **Broader Impact**
   - Product serves non-critical domains
   - Research serves critical infrastructure
   - Both validate TELOS framework
   - Product funds research continuation
