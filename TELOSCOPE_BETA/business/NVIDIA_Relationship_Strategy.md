### Track 2: Acquisition Option (SECONDARY - Long Term)

**Goal:** Keep door open for NVIDIA acquisition IF strategic fit emerges

**Timeline:** March 2026 - June 2026+ (revisit after platform partnerships prove value)

**When to Activate Track 2:**

Only consider shifting focus to acquisition discussions if:
1. ✅ TELOS has secured at least ONE major platform partnership (LangChain, Anthropic, or Microsoft)
2. ✅ Pilot data proves measurable value (drift detection, compliance automation)
3. ✅ NVIDIA proactively expresses acquisition interest
4. ✅ Acquisition would accelerate mission faster than independent growth

**Decision Criteria:**

| Factor | Partnership Path | Acquisition Path |
|--------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Control** | ✅ Maintain full product control | ❌ NVIDIA dictates roadmap |
| **Speed** | 🟡 Moderate (dependent on partners) | ✅ Instant distribution via NVIDIA |
| **Revenue Potential** | ✅ High (20-40% of partner revenue) | 🟡 One-time payout + earnout |
| **Mission Alignment** | ✅ Pure governance focus | 🟡 NVIDIA's broader strategy may dilute |
| **Risk** | 🟡 Partner dependencies | ✅ Financial security |

**Valuation Considerations:**

Do NOT discuss acquisition valuation until:
- TELOS has live production deployments with 3+ enterprise customers
- Provable metrics: $X saved in compliance costs, Y drift events detected
- Partnership revenue is either:
  - Contracted (not just MOU/pilot)
  - OR demonstrably scalable (pilot → full deployment conversion)

**Minimum Acceptable Acquisition Terms (if discussions begin):**
- Valuation reflects platform partnership potential (not just current revenue)
- Earnout tied to governance adoption metrics (not NVIDIA's unrelated goals)
- Jeffrey remains as product lead for TELOS (at least 2 years)
- TELOS maintains independent brand (governance credibility requires neutrality)

- Build TELOS → NVIDIA NIM integration

**Scenario 2: Multiple Platform Partnerships Secured (Best Case)**

**Situation:**
- LangChain partnership live (Q1 2026)
- Anthropic partnership signed (Q2 2026)
- Microsoft Copilot Studio discussions advanced
- $100K+ MRR from platform partnerships

**NVIDIA Strategy:**
- **Consider Track 2 (Acquisition) IF:**

  - Valuation reflects platform partnership value (not just current revenue)

  - NVIDIA becomes 4th platform partner (alongside LangChain, Anthropic, Microsoft)
  - TELOS remains independent governance layer across ecosystems

**Scenario 3: Platform Partnerships Stall (Contingency)**

**Situation:**
- LangChain pilot inconclusive or they pass
- Anthropic/Microsoft discussions slow
- TELOS has technical validation but no distribution

**NVIDIA Strategy:**
- **Accelerate Track 2 (Acquisition)**
- Reason: Without platform partnerships, NVIDIA acquisition may be best path to scale
- Approach: "TELOS has proven governance technology, NVIDIA has distribution"
- Valuation expectations: Lower (no partnership traction) but still strategic value

---

**Scenario 4: NVIDIA Acquires Competitor (Strategic Threat)**

**Situation:**
- NVIDIA acquires another agentic AI governance startup

**TELOS Strategy:**
- **Pivot to platform-agnostic positioning**
- Emphasize independence: "NVIDIA-owned governance creates conflict of interest"
- Focus on LangChain, Anthropic, Microsoft partnerships (non-NVIDIA ecosystem)
- Position TELOS as "neutral third-party governance" (like SOC 2 auditors)

**During Inception Application:**
> "TELOS provides Primacy State governance for agentic AI systems. We're building partnerships with major agentic AI platforms (LangChain, Anthropic) to embed governance by default. NVIDIA NIM is a natural integration point - governed inference as infrastructure."

**If Asked About Acquisition Interest:**

**If Asked About Competitors:**
> "We're not aware of production-grade governance solutions using mathematical frameworks like Primacy State. Most 'AI safety' tools are post-hoc auditing, not real-time intervention. TELOS is the only system with Lyapunov stability validation."

### What NOT to Say to NVIDIA

**Avoid:**
- ❌ "We're looking to get acquired" (seems desperate, reduces leverage)
- ❌ "NVIDIA is our only path to scale" (untrue, weakens negotiating position)
- ❌ Specific customer names without permission (violates confidentiality)
- ❌ "We're close to running out of money" (red flag for any partner/acquirer)
- ❌ Criticizing NVIDIA's existing offerings (positions TELOS as adversarial)

**Instead:**
- ✅ "We're building the governance layer for the agentic AI ecosystem"
- ✅ "NVIDIA NIM + TELOS = governed inference infrastructure"
- ✅ "Platform partnerships are proving strong demand for bundled governance"
- ✅ "We're well-funded through our pilot phase" (even if bootstrapped)
- ✅ "TELOS complements NVIDIA's inference platform - not competes"
