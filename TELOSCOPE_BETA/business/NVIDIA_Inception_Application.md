## Problem Statement

### The Agentic AI Alignment Gap

Current agentic AI systems face three critical challenges:

1. **Purpose Drift**: Agents gradually deviate from their original objectives over extended interactions
2. **Coherence Decay**: Multi-turn conversations lose contextual integrity
3. **Undetectable Misalignment**: No real-time monitoring of agent fidelity to stated goals

**Industry Impact:**
- **Finance**: Agents making trades misaligned with client risk tolerance
- **Healthcare**: Recommendation systems drifting from treatment protocols
- **Legal**: Advisory agents straying from fiduciary obligations
- **Enterprise**: Customer service agents violating brand guidelines

**The Cost:** Each misalignment event represents regulatory risk, liability exposure, and brand damage.

## Solution: TELOS Agentic AI Observatory

TELOS is an autonomous alignment monitoring system with three core capabilities:

### 2. Real-Time Fidelity Scoring
- Continuous measurement of agent adherence to extracted purpose

- **Observatory Lens**: Turn-by-turn analysis of alignment trends

### Primacy Attractor Mathematics

[Summary of PA framework - the mathematical model that makes TELOS unique]

The Primacy Attractor represents the "gravitational center" of a stated purpose, modeled as a vector space where:
- Each user utterance creates a semantic vector
- PA extraction identifies the central attractor basin
- Drift is measured as deviation from this attractor
- Fidelity scores quantify alignment strength

### Fidelity Scoring Algorithm

Dual-axis measurement:
1. **Semantic Drift**: Cosine similarity between current state and PA
2. **Coherence Decay**: Information entropy across turn history

Combined into unified fidelity score: `F = α·(1 - drift) + β·coherence`
