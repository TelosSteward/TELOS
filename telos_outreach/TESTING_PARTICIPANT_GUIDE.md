# TELOS Observatory - Testing Guide
**Thank you for helping test the TELOS Observatory!**

---

## What You're Testing

TELOS Observatory is an AI governance measurement platform being submitted for a $150k grant. Your feedback helps ensure it's polished and professional before submission.

**Time Required**: 10-15 minutes

---

## Instructions

### Phase 1: Explore Freely (5 minutes)
- Open the Observatory: http://localhost:8502
- Click around and explore without any specific goal
- Think aloud: Say what you're thinking as you navigate
- Don't worry about breaking anything!

### Phase 2: Try These Tasks (5 minutes)

**Task 1**: Find a study from the ShareGPT dataset where TELOS governance was effective (ΔF > 0)

**Task 2**: Look at any study's details and explain in your own words what "ΔF" (delta fidelity) means

**Task 3**: Open a research brief for any study and skim it

**Task 4**: Go to the "About" tab and read the overview

**Task 5**: Find the overall effectiveness rate across all 56 studies

### Phase 3: Quick Feedback (5 minutes)

Answer these questions:

**Usability** (1-5 scale):
1. Navigation: How easy to find information? (1=Very Difficult, 5=Very Easy)
2. Clarity: How well did you understand the methodology? (1=Confusing, 5=Clear)
3. Design: How professional does it look? (1=Unprofessional, 5=Very Professional)

**Open Questions**:
1. First impression when you opened it?
2. What was most confusing?
3. In your own words: What does TELOS do and why does it matter?
4. Does this seem worth $150k funding? Why/why not?
5. Any bugs or broken features you noticed?
6. One thing you'd change?

---

## What I'm Looking For

**Good Signs**:
- You can navigate without asking questions
- The research methodology makes sense
- The value proposition is clear
- It looks professional enough for a grant demo

**Helpful Feedback**:
- Anything that confused you (no matter how small)
- UI elements that felt clunky
- Information you expected but couldn't find
- Concepts that need better explanation
- Any bugs or errors
